rebranding myself spring'17

in the beginning.

It all began with a hardcore brainstorming session. I thought to myself, what kind of designer are you? What kind of message do you want to send to the people visiting your site? The answers to these questions were not so simple. So, I began looking online for inspiration.

what i didn't love.

While I'm all about small details and busy artwork, I appreciate clean and intuitive web interfaces. I started building my site with a bootstrap theme, but I began to take issue with it, because I wanted something more simple.

i finally had it.

I decided to completely scrap my bootstrapped site, and started building from scratch.

I love a thoughtful use of whitespace, so I wanted to make sure that I had just the right amount in my new site. I began experimenting with font pairings, colour schemes, and animations. Eventually, it all came down to this.
